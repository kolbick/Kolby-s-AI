Kolby’s AI

The Story Behind Kolby’s AI

What began as a scrappy demo is now a connected cockpit. Kolby spent months coding late nights, wiring clinical workflows, and shipping proposals to clients. The backend is live, and the frontend finally tells the story.

Purple + gold Backend connected Therapist-first

Why it started

Kolby wanted an assistant that spoke the language of therapists: fast SOAP notes, discharge summaries, and treatment plans that don’t feel like chores. The first prototype was a bare-bones static site. It worked for demos but never reflected the polish or ambition behind the project.

Now a real, connected frontend

Backend is online

The UI calls the configured OpenWebUI endpoint with the JWT stored in the repo’s configuration. Every chat you see in the cockpit is powered by that live backend.

Designed for clinicians

Navigation highlights clinical actions—new chat, models, notes, plans, and discharge summaries—so workflows feel cohesive instead of patched together.

Mobile matters

The refreshed layout keeps the chat input usable above the keyboard, adds a reliable hamburger menu, and keeps buttons comfortably tappable down to 320px.

The vision

Kolby’s AI is built to slot into therapist workflows: capture a note, summarize progress, and hand off a treatment plan without breaking focus. The pirate logo stays playful, but the mission is serious—help clinicians reclaim their time while keeping clinical quality front and center.
